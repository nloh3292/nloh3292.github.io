I am a passionate Data Scientist with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and a Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence. My strong background lies in data analysis and machine learning techniques to develop innovative solutions that improve people's quality of life.

Throughout my academic journey, I have gained proficiency in various programming languages such as Python, R, and SQL, as well as hands-on experience with data science frameworks like Pandas, NumPy, and scikit-learn. My projects include predicting COVID-19 based on patient symptoms, fingerprint detection and recognition, and wildfire detection using RGB images. Additionally, I have contributed to synthetic biology research aimed at developing bacteriophages to combat biofilms from Pseudomonas aeruginosa and Escherichia coli.

I have a deep passion for various industries, particularly in healthcare and the medical field, and I continuously seek opportunities to learn and apply my skills to benefit others.
